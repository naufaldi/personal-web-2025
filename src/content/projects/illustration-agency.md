## Overview

A sophisticated landing page for an Illustration Agency, designed to serve as a comprehensive digital storefront. The site showcases the agency's portfolio, services, and capabilities while seamlessly integrating with Google Sheets for lead management.

## Purpose

The landing page was created to:

- **Showcase Portfolio**: Display illustration work and capabilities
- **Attract Clients**: Engage potential clients with compelling design
- **Generate Leads**: Capture contact information effectively
- **Build Authority**: Establish the agency as a leader in illustration

## Tech Stack

- **React**: Component-based UI framework
- **Next.js**: Production-ready React framework
- **Google Sheets API**: Data collection and management
- **Tailwind CSS**: Modern styling approach

## Key Features

### Portfolio Showcase

- High-quality image galleries
- Project descriptions and details
- Client success stories
- Visual design excellence

### Contact Integration

- Contact forms with validation
- Direct integration with Google Sheets
- Automated data collection
- Lead tracking capabilities

### Lead Management

- Seamless data capture from visitors
- Automatic organization in Google Sheets
- Easy follow-up workflows
- Lead qualification tracking

### Responsive Design

- Mobile-friendly interface
- Optimized for all device sizes
- Fast load times
- Excellent user experience

## Lead Generation

The Google Sheets integration enables:

- **Real-time Data Collection**: Captures leads as they're submitted
- **Automatic Organization**: Organizes contact information systematically
- **Easy Accessibility**: Team access through Google Drive
- **Simple Follow-up**: Quick access to prospect information
