## Overview

The Logos Festival landing page showcases an inclusive educational event organized by Logos NGO. Centered on the theme of Justice, the festival brings together leading academicians and international thinkers for a series of enlightening lectures and discussions exploring justice and political philosophy.

## Event Overview

**Dates**: November 3 - December 4

The festival is structured around two main components:

- **Justice Bootcamp**: Intensive educational program
- **Justice Talks**: Expert discussion series

## Event Objectives

The festival aims to:

- **Educate**: Provide comprehensive education on justice topics
- **Engage**: Foster dialogue among diverse participants
- **Inspire**: Encourage critical thinking about justice and philosophy
- **Connect**: Build community around shared values

## Event Structure

### Justice Bootcamp

An intensive educational program covering:

- **Legal Principles**: Foundations of justice systems
- **Political Philosophy**: Theoretical frameworks
- **Case Studies**: Real-world justice issues
- **Practical Applications**: How philosophy translates to action

### Justice Talks

A series of expert discussions featuring:

- **Renowned Academicians**: Leading scholars in their fields
- **International Thinkers**: Global perspectives on justice
- **Interactive Sessions**: Q&A with audience participation
- **Networking**: Opportunities to connect with other participants

## Featured Topics

The festival explores:

- Social justice and equality
- Legal systems and reform
- International law and human rights
- Political philosophy and governance
- Ethics in modern society

## Speaker Lineup

The festival features:

- **Expert Academicians**: Top researchers and scholars
- **International Thinkers**: Leaders from around the world
- **Practitioners**: Professionals applying justice in the field
- **Activists**: Leaders in social justice movements
