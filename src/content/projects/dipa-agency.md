## Overview

Dipa Agency's landing page is a vibrant digital presence that showcases the agency's portfolio, team, and capabilities. Built with Next.js for optimal performance and user experience, the site is designed to impress potential clients and generate business opportunities.

## Project Objectives

The landing page aims to:

- **Showcase Portfolio**: Display completed projects and case studies
- **Introduce Team**: Highlight team members and expertise
- **Build Trust**: Establish credibility and agency authority
- **Generate Leads**: Attract and convert potential clients

## Tech Stack

- **Next.js**: React framework for server-side rendering
- **React**: Component-based architecture
- **TypeScript**: Type-safe development
- **Tailwind CSS**: Utility-first styling
